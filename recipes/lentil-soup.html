Lentil Soup

Home

Eat lentils. Do it.

Ingredients

  • pound sweet Italian pork sausage, cut into 1/2-inch slices
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 1 stalk celery, finely chopped
  • 1 tablespoon chopped garlic
  • 8 cups water
  • 2 (14.5 ounce) cans chicken broth
  • 1 (28 ounce) can diced tomatoes
  • 1 (16 ounce) package dry lentils, rinsed
  • 1 cup shredded carrot
  • 1 tablespoon garlic powder/li>
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 tablespoon salt, or to taste
  • ½ teaspoon black pepper
  • ½ teaspoon dried oregano
  • ¼ teaspoon dried thyme
  • ¼ teaspoon dried basil
  • 2 large bay leaves
  • ½ pound ditalini pasta (Optional)

Steps

  1. Cook sausage in a large pot over medium-high heat until evenly brown, 7 to 10 minutes.
  2. Add onion, celery, and garlic; sauté until tender and translucent, about 5 minutes.
  3. Stir in water, chicken broth, tomatoes, lentils, carrot, garlic powder, parsley, salt, pepper, oregano, thyme, basil, and bay leaves; bring to a boil. Reduce the heat, cover, and simmer until lentils are tender, 2 1/2 to 3 hours.
  4. Stir in pasta and cook until tender, 15 to 20 minutes. Remove bay leaves before serving.
